About Katie

A dietitian who answers the phone herself.

Katie Sengheiser is a Registered Dietitian licensed in Missouri and New Jersey, practicing entirely by telehealth from St. Peters, Missouri. Her clinical focus is weight management and bariatric nutrition, with a genuine soft spot for helping kids and teens build a workable relationship with food.

She built a solo practice on purpose: the person who takes your referral, verifies the coverage, and sits across the screen at every visit is the same person.

— Katie Sengheiser, RD
