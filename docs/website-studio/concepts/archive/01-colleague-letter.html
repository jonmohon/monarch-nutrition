Motion

Quiet, once, on paper.

Entrances rise 40px over 700ms on a soft-out curve, staggered 80ms — the hero above demonstrates it on load; service rows and fee cards use scroll-driven timelines where supported. Hovers are weight shifts and 1px lifts, never bounces. The chart rail on the panel edge scrubs with scroll. All of it collapses to rest states under prefers-reduced-motion. The full build adds IntersectionObserver-gated reveals for all browsers and an active-section indicator on the rail — no animation library needed.
