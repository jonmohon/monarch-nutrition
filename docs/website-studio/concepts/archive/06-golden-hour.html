About Katie

The same person, at every step.

Katie Sengheiser is a Registered Dietitian licensed in Missouri and New Jersey, practicing entirely by telehealth from St. Peters, Missouri. Her clinical focus is weight management and bariatric nutrition, with a particular devotion to helping children and teens build an easy relationship with food.

She keeps the practice deliberately small: the person who answers your first call, verifies your coverage, and sits across the screen at every visit is the same clinician — by design.

— Katie Sengheiser, RD
